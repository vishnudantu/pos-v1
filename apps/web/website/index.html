FAQ

Common questions

Is my constituency data secure and private?▼
Yes. ThoughtFirst is fully self-hosted on your own dedicated VPS server — we never store your data on our servers. Voter records, grievances, WhatsApp messages, financial data — everything stays on your private server in Singapore or India. We have no access to your political data at any time.
Does it work in Telugu and Hindi?▼
Yes. The AI assistant generates content in English, Telugu, and Hindi. WhatsApp messages in Telugu are automatically detected and processed. Field workers can submit voice reports in Telugu and Hindi — automatically transcribed by Whisper AI. The morning brief can be delivered in Telugu on WhatsApp.
How does the WhatsApp Intelligence work?▼
Your party workers and staff forward WhatsApp messages to a dedicated intelligence number linked to AiSensy or Wati. The platform automatically classifies each message — grievance, emergency, misinformation, opposition activity, or praise — and routes it to the right module. Viral content (same message from 5 or more different people) is flagged immediately with an urgent notification.
What is the Temple Darshan module?▼
MPs and MLAs have a quota for VIP darshan at Tirupati, Shirdi, Vaishno Devi, Char Dham, and other major temples. ThoughtFirst manages the entire process — constituent applications, eligibility and cooldown checks, approval workflow, automatic SMS confirmation to pilgrims, and booking letter generation. Covers 50+ temples across India with temple-specific rules and quota management.
How quickly can we get started?▼
Within 48 hours of payment confirmation. We deploy the platform on a dedicated server, configure SSL, set up your profile with AI auto-fill from public data, and train your team. WhatsApp integration takes one additional day. SMS setup is immediate once you share your Fast2SMS key.
Does it work for MLAs and Mayors too, not just MPs?▼
Yes. The platform auto-configures based on designation — MP (Lok Sabha), MP (Rajya Sabha), MLA, MLC, Mayor, or Municipal Councillor. Each gets different modules and widgets. An MLA's dashboard focuses on state legislation and assembly constituency; an MP's focuses on Sansad.in and MPLADS. MLCs in AP, Telangana, Maharashtra, UP, Karnataka, and Bihar are fully supported.
What AI models power the platform?▼
The platform supports Gemini (Google), Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4o (OpenAI), and Llama 3.3 via Groq. You bring your own API key — there is no additional AI billing from us. The Starter plan uses Groq (free tier), which is fast and reliable. Professional and above plans typically use Gemini 1.5 Flash or Claude Haiku for best results.
